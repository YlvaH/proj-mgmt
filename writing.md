# Writing
## Steps in the process

1. Write paper/article
2. Make tables, graphs & figures
3. Submit
4. Wait
5a. Get accepted -> 6
5b. Get rejected -> 7
6. Celebrate, have a beer & a break
7. Weep, revise & resubmit
8. Wait some more
9. Get accepted and celbrate
10. Reflection - What went wrong and how to improve?
